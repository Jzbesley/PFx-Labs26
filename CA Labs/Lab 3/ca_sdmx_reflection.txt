CA Lab 3 — SDMX Discussion Notes
============================================================

California uses the Local Control Funding Formula (LCFF)
to categorize student need. This is more granular than
a simple yes/no meals program flag — it recognizes English
learners, foster youth, and low-income students separately.

Discussion: How does LCFF compare to SDMX standards?
What categories does LCFF still oversimplify?
