WHAT IS SDMX?
SDMX stands for Statistical Data and Metadata eXchange. It is a
shared rulebook used by organizations like the World Bank, the UN,
and national statistics agencies so that when different countries
share data, their categories mean the same thing.

For example: one country might count "low income" as anyone earning
under $15,000/year; another might use $25,000. Without a shared
standard, you can't fairly compare data across those two countries.
